About Us

TinkerHat Foundation started working in 2020 and officially registered TinkerHat in 2021. In last 1 year they have established schools with programs in 5 states. Student beneficiaries - 9000+, Teaching staff- 100+, 31 schools. Their current goal is to have a volunteer program in place where they can bridge the gap of accessibility by providing a teacher volunteer. They are partnered with STEAM EDUCATION which is an innovative approach to learning that uses Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Maths. They conduct various coding workshops, Innovation and Tinkering Lab sessions which benefits the students.
